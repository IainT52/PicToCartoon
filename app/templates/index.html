What happens when a Neural Network
tries to draw your photo?

A fun project made by Iain Tierney.

PicToCartoon uses TensorFlow 2 Neural Networks and Google QuickDraw (millions of cartoon drawings made by real people) to detect and recreate a doodle of common objects in a picture.
